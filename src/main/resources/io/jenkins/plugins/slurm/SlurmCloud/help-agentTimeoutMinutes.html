Maximum time to wait for a Slurm job to start and the Jenkins agent to become available.

This timeout covers the entire process from job submission to agent connection:

  • Job queuing time in Slurm
  • Node allocation and job startup
  • SSH connection establishment
  • Jenkins agent initialization

Recommended values:

  • 30-60 minutes - Busy clusters with queuing
  • 10-15 minutes - Dedicated or lightly loaded clusters
  • 5-10 minutes - Testing or development environments
